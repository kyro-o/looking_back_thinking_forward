Thinking Forward

Next year, I will be attending Stony Brook University in Stony Brook, New York. Out of all the schools that accepted me, I settled with Stony Brook mostly due to its location and ranking. I'm planning to study Business Management, focusing on Human Resources or Supply Chain Management. In the coming year, the thing I am looking forward to the most is moving and studying in an environment I miss. The busy, bustling, city life is something I enjoy a lot. Many people like to have peace and quiet living in suburban areas, however, city life is more fitting for me. Another reason is that I have not been back to my birth state in about 10 years.

After college, my plan is to go into the field of Human Resource or Supply Chain Management. During my time in school, I'll be exercising my resources to obtain internships or jobs in my focus field. I am not planning for a big company, but am hoping to get into a decent company. I also plan to travel around the world to places I hope to visit with friends before jumping straight into work, but that plan is for later. Some locations are shown in the image slideshow below.
